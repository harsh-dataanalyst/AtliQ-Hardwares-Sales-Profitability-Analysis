# AtliQ-Hardwares-Sales-Profitability-Analysis 
I recently worked on a sales and profitability analysis for AtliQ Hardwares, leveraging Excel, Power Pivot, and Data Modeling to uncover key business insights. This project allowed me to apply advanced analytics techniques to solve real-world business problems.

Problem Statement:

Understanding gross margin (GM%) trends, sales performance, and profitability across different markets and fiscal years is crucial for making data-driven business decisions. However, the challenge lies in handling large datasets efficiently and deriving meaningful insights.

How I Solved It:

✅ Used Power Pivot & Data Modeling to handle large datasets, establish relationships between tables, and create dynamic reports.
✅ Built interactive dashboards in Excel to visualize trends and compare GM% across regions and time periods.
✅ Analyzed Year-over-Year (YoY) sales performance to identify growth opportunities and areas of concern.
✅ Identified cost inefficiencies by examining COGS trends and their impact on gross margins.

Key Findings & Insights:

📌 GM% Analysis (2019-2021):

Markets like ANZ and SE consistently maintained strong GM%, indicating pricing power or cost efficiencies.

India saw a significant drop in GM% in 2020, possibly due to increased costs or competitive pressures.


📌 Sales & Profitability Trends:

March 2021 had a massive 1981.6% YoY growth, suggesting a demand surge or operational improvements.

The USA and UK had the highest net sales, while Germany and Austria struggled with lower GM%, highlighting potential pricing or cost issues.


📌 Profit & Loss Insights:

Q2 of FY 2021 saw the highest net sales, emphasizing seasonal trends or strategic business shifts.

Some regions had a higher cost-to-revenue ratio, affecting profitability and requiring optimization strategies.


Key Takeaways:

🔹 Power Pivot & Data Modeling are game changers for handling and analyzing large datasets efficiently.
🔹 Data visualization in Excel helped in identifying trends and making insights more actionable.
🔹 Data-driven decision-making is essential for optimizing profitability and market strategies.
